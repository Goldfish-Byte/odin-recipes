Vegetarische Spätzle

Description

Tasty vegetable Spätzle

Ingredients

  • Spätzle
  • Cooking Cream
  • Tomato
  • Spinach
  • Italian Mixed Spices
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cut vegetables
  2. Fry Spätzle in a pan
  3. Fry Garlic, Tomato, Spinach in a second pan
  4. Add Cooking Cream and Spices after a while
  5. Mix everything together
