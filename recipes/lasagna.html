Vegetarische Lasagne

Description

Tasty vegetable lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Noodle plates
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Aubergine
  • Zuchini

Steps

  1. Cut vegetables
  2. Put first layer of vegetables
  3. Put layer of noodle plates
  4. Put second layer of vegetables
  5. Put second layer of noodle plates
  6. Put Sauce and cheese on top
  7. Put it in the oven for 30 minutes at 180 degree
