Vegetarische Pizza

Description

Tasty vegetable Pizza.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Ready made pizza dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Zuchini
  • Paprika
  • Spinat

Steps

  1. Cut vegetables
  2. Roll out Pizza dough
  3. Put Tomato Sauce on it
  4. Put cut vegetables on it
  5. Put cheese on it
  6. Put it in the oven for 20 minutes at 180 degree
